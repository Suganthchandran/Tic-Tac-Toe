Instructions

1. Enter the names of the players and the points required to win the game.
2. Player 1 starts as 'X' and Player 2 as 'O'.
3. Players take turns clicking on the cells to mark their symbols.
4. The first player to align three symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins the round.
5. The player who wins a round gets a point.
6. After each round, the symbols switch: Player 1 will play as 'O' and Player 2 as 'X'.
7. The game ends when one player reaches the required points to win.
8. Click 'New Game' to start a new game, which will require confirmation.
